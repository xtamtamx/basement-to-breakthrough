Settling Up — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 June 2026

Settling Up is a premium, offline-first game. The short version: we don't collect, transmit, or sell any of your data. There are no ads, no analytics, no trackers, and no third-party SDKs watching you play.

What we collect

Nothing. We do not run analytics or telemetry, and the game has no user accounts.

Your game data

  • Your saves, settings, and progress are stored locally on your device (browser storage / on-device app storage).
  • This data never leaves your device. We can't see it, and there is no server it syncs to.
  • You can erase it any time by clearing the app's data or deleting the app.

No tracking, no ads, no in-app purchases

  • No advertising or ad networks.
  • No usage analytics, fingerprinting, or behavioral tracking.
  • No in-app purchases — the game is a one-time premium purchase.

Third-party services

The game loads a web font from Google Fonts when run in a browser; your browser's IP address is necessarily visible to that service as part of any web request. The installed app caches fonts locally and works fully offline. No other third-party services are used.

Children's privacy

Settling Up does not collect personal information from anyone, including children.
